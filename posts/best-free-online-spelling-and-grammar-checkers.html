Check Spelling and Grammar Of Your Text Online

Before posting any text in your blog, it should be correct in terms of Spelling and Grammar. Text which are correctly written with correct spelling and grammar can be easily understood by your post readers. Also it will help them to keep interest on reading your posts.

If your post contains a lot of spelling mistakes as well as grammatical errors, it will be difficult for your visitors to understand your posts.

Here, I'm going to share some free online tools that can be used for checking spelling, grammar, syntax, etc. and make your post free from any grammatical errors.

1. online-spellcheck.com

Online-spellcheck is a free and easy to use online spelling and grammar checker. You can either enter a text to check the spelling or enter URL of a web page or upload a text file or image file with text or even the files from your cloud storage like Google Drive or Dropbox.

How to use:

  1. Open online-spellcheck.com on your browser.
  2. Enter your Text to check the spelling.
  3. Click on Check Text.
  4. It will show you all the spelling and grammatical errors on your text.
  5. From the result, you can detect the mistake on your text and correct it.

2. reverso.net

Reverso.net is also a good and free spelling and grammar checker for English texts.

How to use:

  1. Open reverso.net/spell-checker/english-spelling-grammar in your browser.
  2. Enter your English text in the text box.
  3. Click on Check.
  4. It will correct the spelling and grammar mistakes on your text and mark the automatically corrected ones in blue-underlined font.
  5. You will get an error-free text after correcting all the mistakes.

3. grammarcheck.net

Grammarcheck.net is also a good and free as well as paid spelling and grammar checker. Along with the spelling and grammar checking, this tool can also detect and correct the wrong use of very similar meaning words. Like using accurate instead of correct. Both the words have similar meaning but it can detect which one will be perfect to use in that case.

How to use:

  1. Open grammarcheck.net/editor on your browser.
  2. Enter your English text to check the spelling and grammatical errors.
  3. Click on Free Check button.
  4. Click on the text with underline and click on the correct suggestion to correct it.
  5. After correcting all the text underlined, you will get an error free text.

Conclusion

To write a text without error is difficult job, even for experts. The bloggers, writers, etc. should write error free text. But it is difficult to notice where you've got error and what's the correct form of it. So, after using one of the above tools, you can write an error free text on your blog or your pages.
