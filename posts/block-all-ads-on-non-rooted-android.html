Block All Ads On Apps and Websites on Non-Rooted Android

Are you irritated of those annoying ads and want to get rid of them? If yes, this tutorial is going to be very helpful for you. In this tutorial, you will learn how you can block ads on your apps and website without rooting your Android device.

In the previous article I had written on how you can block ads on your rooted Android device. But everyone who want to block ads don't have rooted phone. So, for them, that tutorial was not of any use. So, to overcome it, I found some ways to block all ads on your phone without rooting your device.

There are lots of apps that can block ads without root. Here, I've discussed about most popular, easy to use and useful one. It doesn't matter whether your phone is rooted or not, these methods work for both rooted and non-rooted device.

Advantages of using Adblocker

  1. Increases your browsing experience.
  2. Get rid of the annoying ads.
  3. Prevents the collection of your personal data by Ad Servers.
  4. Reduce your data consumption and many more.

Limitations of using Adblocker

  1. The ad revenue of a developer gets reduced.
  2. You won't be able to access some apps and websites that use adblocker detection.
  3. You wont't be able to get rewards or unlock a feature in games or apps by watching video ad after using an Adblocker.

1. Block This!

Block This! is a free and open-source adblocker for Android which can block ads on all apps and browsers. In addition to blocking ads, it can also provides more features like malware protection, faster and safer browsing, less data consumption, etc. To read more about the app and download it, go to its official site.

Main Features

  1. Block all ads on apps and website including audio ads, video ads, popup ads, banner ads, etc.
  2. Malware protection.
  3. Save your mobile data upto 35% just by blocking all type of ads and many more.
  4. No root required.

How to use Block This:

  1. First of all, you need to download and install Block This! app. Click here to go to the official site to download the app.
  2. After downloading, install and open the app.
    You might need to turn on Unknown Sources in security settings to be able to install app from third party sources.
  3. After opening the app, click the button to start ad blocking. It will ask for permission to create a VPN, click OK.
  4. You will see a VPN icon(a key like icon) on your status bar if the adblocking service is working properly. Once ad blocking servive is started successfully, you won't see any ads on apps and websites.
  5. If you want to ignore certain app from adblocking, you can whitelist it by Click On Hamburger Menu > Whitelist > + and choose an you want to put in a whitelist.

Block This! is an easy-to-use, and useful app that can block ads on your android device without rooting your device.

2. Browsers with adblocker feature

There are lots of browsers like Opera, Brave, Via, etc. which come with built-in adblocker feature. These browsers can block all ads, popups, etc. from the webpage and serve adfree webpage. You can easily exclude any website from adblocking if you don't want to block ads on certain websites.


Conclusion

Following this tutorial, you have learnt how you can block all ads on your website and apps without rooting your Android Device.
