Change Screen Resolution Of Your Android Device

Are you tired of those huge fonts, images and small screen resolution of your Android Phone? If Yes, this article is going to be very useful for you. Here, I'm going to show how you can easily customize the default screen resolution of your Android Phone.

For this, you must have your Android phone rooted, than only you can install and run the app which we are going to use in this tutorial.

If you are already rooted, than you can step forward to download the app.

Steps to change your screen resolution.

  1. Download and install Easy DPI Changer app from Play Store. Click here to download the app.
  2. Open the app and it will ask for root permission. Grant the root permission.
  3. After the app gets the root permission, you will come to the app homepage. You will see all the values are Default:
  4. Than go to Resolution and enter the values you want to apply. In this step you need to be very careful while chosing the width(horizontal) and height(vertical) resolution values. You need to set it according to your device's screen aspect ratio. Set the required values and press Apply.

    Do not use very high value. Try to set it below 2160 * 3840. Also depending upon your screen size. Set values nearer to the default settings for better experience.
  5. After applying, you might receive a warning box. Press Yes and than you are all done.

After changing your device resolution, you might not be able to capture screenshot or record screen in normal process.

Reverting your screen recolution to original state

If you get any problem with your screen, icons, font, etc. after using this or anything else, you can easily revert it to the original at one click. To so so, follow these steps.

  1. Open the "Easy DPI Changer" app.
  2. Click Reset button and click Yes to confirm.

After changing your screen resolution to default, your screen will again change to normal like as it is before.

After changing your screen resolution to default, you will again be able to take screenshots and screen record normally like as before.

Conclusion

After using the Easy DPI Changer app, you will be able to customize your Screen Resolution, Padding(black space on the side) and DPI in a way what you like. But while changing these, do not set too high or too low values, try to set nearer values depending upon your default screen resolution.
